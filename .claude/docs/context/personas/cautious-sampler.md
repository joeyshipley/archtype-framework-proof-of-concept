# Sofia - The Cautious Sampler (Free Tier)

## Demographics

- **Age:** 10 years old (4th grade)
- **Location:** Rural area, Texas
- **Tech Savvy:** Low-moderate (shared family tablet)
- **Reading Level:** Slightly below grade level

## Background

- Still building reading confidence
- Family budget-conscious, unlikely to subscribe
- Needs gentle onboarding
- Prefers familiar, comfortable stories

## Goals

- Find stories at her reading level
- Not feel overwhelmed by complexity
- Complete at least one full trilogy
- Feel good about her choices

## Pain Points

- Intimidated by too many options
- Worried about making "wrong" choices
- Afraid of losing progress
- Doesn't understand gamification elements immediately

## Behaviors

- Reads sporadically (once a week if reminded)
- Sticks with one series for long periods
- Rarely switches or explores new series
- Needs encouragement to complete

## Needs from Platform

- Simple, clear interface
- Forgiving choice system
- Reading level indicators
- Encouraging feedback (not just achievements)

## Quote

> "I just want a good story that I can finish without getting lost."
