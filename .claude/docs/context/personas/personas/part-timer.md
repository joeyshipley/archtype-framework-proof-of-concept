# Maria - The Part-Timer (Casual Author)

## Demographics

- **Age:** 28 years old
- **Background:** Marketing professional, writes for fun
- **Tech Savvy:** Moderate
- **Writing Experience:** Low-moderate (fan fiction, blog posts)

## Background

- Discovered platform through friend (Rachel)
- Writes when inspired, not on schedule
- Treats it as creative outlet, not career
- Has one series idea she's passionate about

## Goals

- Complete one trilogy she's proud of
- See if readers enjoy her work
- Have fun creating without pressure
- Learn writing skills through doing

## Pain Points

- Inconsistent availability to write
- Worried about commitment expectations
- Needs flexibility if life gets busy
- Doesn't want to let readers down

## Behaviors

- Writes in bursts (3 chapters at once, then nothing for weeks)
- Needs reminders and encouragement
- Values community over competition
- Prefers collaborative rather than solo

## Needs from Platform

- Flexible deadlines
- Clear communication about expectations
- Ability to pause/resume projects
- Supportive author community

## Quote

> "I want to create something cool, but I can't treat this like a job."
