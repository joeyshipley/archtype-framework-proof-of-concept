# Tyler - The Social Reader (Teen, YA Content)

## Demographics

- **Age:** 15 years old (9th grade)
- **Location:** Suburban area, Florida
- **Tech Savvy:** Very high (phone-first user)
- **Reading Level:** Grade-appropriate

## Background

- Reads primarily on phone during commute
- Influenced by friends' recommendations
- Active on social media
- Interested in more mature themes (but age-appropriate)

## Goals

- Find series friends are talking about
- Share cool moments on social media
- Access content that feels "grown up" but safe
- Quick entertainment during downtime

## Pain Points

- Kid-focused content feels too young
- Desktop-only experiences don't work for him
- Wants to share/screenshot easily
- Impatient with slow pacing

## Behaviors

- Reads in 10-15 minute bursts throughout day
- Very social - shares everything
- Quick to abandon if not immediately hooked
- Responds to FOMO (limited-time content)

## Needs from Platform

- Mobile-optimized experience
- Age-appropriate but mature content
- Easy sharing features
- Rotation creates urgency

## Quote

> "If it's not on my phone and my friends aren't reading it, I'm not interested."
