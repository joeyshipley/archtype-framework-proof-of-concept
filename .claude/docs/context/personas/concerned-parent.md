# Jennifer - The Concerned Parent

## Demographics

- **Age:** 38 years old
- **Children:** Two kids (9 and 11)
- **Tech Savvy:** Moderate
- **Income:** Middle class

## Background

- Wants kids to read more, less screen time for games
- Worried about online safety and predatory practices
- Monitors what kids access online
- Will pay for quality educational content

## Goals

- Find safe, enriching content for kids
- Encourage reading without being preachy
- Know what kids are exposed to
- Avoid manipulative monetization

## Pain Points

- Skeptical of "free" platforms (hidden costs?)
- Worried about data collection
- Concerned about age-inappropriate content leaking through
- Frustrated by predatory monetization (loot boxes, etc.)

## Behaviors

- Researches platforms before allowing kids access
- Reads reviews from other parents
- Sets screen time limits
- Checks kids' activity periodically

## Needs from Platform

- Clear privacy policy
- Transparent pricing
- Age verification they can trust
- Parental controls/visibility

## Quote

> "I want my kids reading more, but I need to know this platform respects them and isn't just trying to extract money from children."
